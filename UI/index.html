Ever wanted to report an issue that needs Government intervention or just stumbled across evidence of corruption and don't know who to report to?
This app is built for that.

Get Started within 5 minutes

1. Register

Navigate to the sign up page where you will have to fill a form to register. This is necessary to keep track of reports you submit.

2. Login

Login with credentials provided during registration to create a report, update or delete a previously created report and view history of reports you have created

3. Create A Report

On the form to create a report you will be asked to identify the nature of the report. You will also provide a title, comment, location, image(optional) and video(optional).

4. Update A Report

Before a report is being investigated, you can update its location and comment. Navigate to your profile page to see a list of reports you can edit.

5. Delete a report

Before a report is being investigated, you can delete it. To do this, Navigate to your profile page to see a list of reports you can delete.

6. Track all your reports

On your profile page, it is easy to keep track of all your reports and the status of each of the reports. Click on view all reports tab to see a list of all reports submitted.
